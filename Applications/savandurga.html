SAVANDURGA

This hill station is situated 60km west of Bangalore, forming a part of the Deccan Plateau and is considered to be one the largest monoliths in Asia. This place is one of the best places to visit in India and is great for a backpacking trip with your best friends to make the most of the forests, caves, water sports and climbing activities present here. It boasts of some top class serious slab climbing routes in the world ranging from 700 to 950 feet of technical climbs with very innovative names such as ‘Simple Monkey day’ or ‘Cloud 9’.
