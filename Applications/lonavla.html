LONAVLA

A small hill station in the Pune district that has the reputation of being haunted! This place is among the best places to visit in India and calls for a night stay not just to enjoy the spooky feel and ambience of the place but also because it is one of the most beautiful sunrise spots in the district. ‘Ati kya Khandala’ can be finally taken into serious consideration with Khandala lying just adjacent to Lonavla.
