What’s great about Gangtok? Everything! From duty free goods to duty free alcohol, the capital of Sikkim is one of the best places to visit in India to chill with a crazy bunch of friends! This place has something to offer to everyone – adventure enthusiasts who can go trekking nearby, shopping enthusiasts, nature lovers and even people who have come to simply chill and have fun. With numerous cafes, pubs, clubs and casinos, this may very well be a mini Las Vegas in India and an alternative to Goa!
